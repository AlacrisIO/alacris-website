About us

Today’s problem with blockchain is that nobody actually “uses” blockchain. How do we get more users onto blockchain?

Alacris OS is a blockchain agnostic platform that turns any developer into a blockchain developer.

  • Today’s hurdles to build on blockchain:
    • It’s hard
    • Platform selection can be confusing
    • It is very risky to deploy DApps
  • Alacris solves for four critical problems developers face:
    • Ease of Development
    • Speed to Market
    • Flexibility
    • Security
